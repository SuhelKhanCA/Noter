Motivation Behind The Project

Despite the huge influence of technology in our daily life, the majority of the students still create their notes manually with pen and paper. While this may work at a lower level, managing these notes becomes a tedious task at higher levels. Additionally, there is a risk of these paper notes being destroyed or lost. To resolve this problem, we decided to construct a web-based application that helps students to create and manage their notes with ease. While web-based note apps already exist and provide good services, our main aim is to increase the convenience for students manifold by building upon these apps as our base.

How To Use This Site

The user or the student must first register to access the system. Once registered, the student can add, remove, and toggle the visibility (public/private) of their notes. They can also view notes available on the public ledger. Each user will have a unique username and password to access their account, and each note will have a unique identifier called NoteId. Similarly, each public note will have a PublicId.

Admin Controls: The admin is provided with login credentials and can view registered users along with their notes' titles and creators. However, the admin cannot view or change the actual note content and has the authority only to remove a user's note.
